About Us

Empowering Skills, Building Futures

Who We Are

Founded in 2022 by Precious Radebe, Empowering The Nation is a skills development initiative based in Johannesburg. We provide short courses designed to equip domestic workers, gardeners, and community members with practical abilities that improve employability and open doors to entrepreneurship.

Our Mission

At Empowering The Nation, we believe that knowledge is the key to opportunity. Our mission is to uplift individuals through accessible training that boosts earning potential, enhances self-confidence, and supports sustainable livelihoods.

What We Offer

We run Six-Week and Six-Month courses covering essential skills such as Child Minding, Cooking, Garden Maintenance, Landscaping, First Aid, Life Skills, and Sewing. These programmes are designed to fit into busy lives and give learners real-world knowledge that makes a difference in their homes, communities, and careers.
