Frequently Asked Questions

Where are your locations?

Empowering The Nation is located at three different locations: in the South of Johannesburg, Soweto, Roodepoort, and Sandton.

How do I join a course?

You can sign up for our courses via our website or our iOS and Android apps. On the website, simply click the "Apply" button in the navigation. On the app, you will be directed to the sign-up page upon launching it.

How many courses can I enroll in?

We currently offer nine courses. Three are six weeks long, and six are six months long. Scholars are welcome to sign up for more than one course at a time.

Can I volunteer for your organization?

Yes! We welcome volunteers. You can sign up to volunteer at one of the locations closest to you by contacting us through our website.

Do you have a mobile app?

Yes, Empowering The Nation is available on both mobile and our website. Our apps can be found on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

How can I track my progress and class schedule?

Once you create a profile, a personalized dashboard is created for you. This dashboard includes a calendar with all scheduled classes from the start to the end of the course, as well as a progress bar to show your current standing.
