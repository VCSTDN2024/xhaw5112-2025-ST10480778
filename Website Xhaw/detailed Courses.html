6 Week Courses

Childminding

This course equips you with the knowledge and confidence to provide safe, nurturing, and developmentally appropriate care for children of different ages. Perfect for aspiring childminders, au pairs, and caregivers.

What you will learn:

  • Child safety, supervision, and first aid basics
  • Age-appropriate nutrition and healthy meal preparation
  • Creating fun, educational, and stimulating activities
  • Understanding child development stages and needs
  • Building positive relationships with children and parents

Ideal for: Parents, babysitters, nannies, au pairs, and anyone interested in starting a child care service.

Career opportunities: Work as a nanny, au pair, daycare assistant, or start your own childminding business.

Price: R750

First Aid

Gain the knowledge and practical skills to respond confidently to medical emergencies at home, work, or in the community. This accredited course is ideal for parents, caregivers, and professionals.

What you will learn:

  • CPR techniques for adults, children, and infants
  • Wound treatment and bleeding control
  • Emergency response to burns, fractures, and choking
  • Recognizing and managing common medical conditions (asthma, seizures, allergic reactions)
  • How to stay calm and provide assistance until professional help arrives

Ideal for: Teachers, security staff, caregivers, parents, and community leaders.

Career opportunities: Health & safety officer, first aid volunteer, workplace first aid provider.
