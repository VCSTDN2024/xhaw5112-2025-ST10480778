About Us

At Empowering The Nation, we believe that knowledge is the key to opportunity. Founded in 2022 by Precious Radebe, we empower domestic workers, gardeners, and members of our community with practical skills that improve employability, increase earning potential, and open doors to entrepreneurship.

OUR STORY

Established in 2022 by Precious Radebe, Empowering the Nation began with a simple belief: knowledge creates opportunity. We design courses with the realities of learners in mind — short, affordable and practical pathways into paid work or self-employment.
