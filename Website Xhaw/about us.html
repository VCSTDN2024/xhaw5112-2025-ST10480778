OUR STORY

Knowledge is the Key to Opportunity

Founded in 2022 by Precious Radebe, Empowering The Nation began with a simple belief: knowledge creates opportunity. We empower domestic workers, gardeners, and members of our community with practical skills that improve employability and open doors to entrepreneurship.

We design our courses with the realities of our learners in mind—offering short, affordable, and practical pathways into paid work or self-employment.
